Product Description

Easy to clean and reusable. Designed to help caregivers change diapers quickly and securely for children wearing a spica cast (hip dysplasia). Soft on skin, with a bendable inner side for gentle support and strong Velcro for secure fastening.

  • Designed specifically for hip dysplasia / spica cast use
  • Allows use of a regular-size diaper and reduces leaks
  • Faster diaper changes — up to twice the speed
  • Soft outer fabric, breathable and skin-friendly
  • Durable, super-adhesive Velcro to secure through plaster

Directions: Place the diaper on the child with a spica cast, secure it with the Velcro, remove it for changing, and clean and reuse as necessary.
